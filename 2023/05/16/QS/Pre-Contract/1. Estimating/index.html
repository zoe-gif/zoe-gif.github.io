General of Estimating

Purpose

  • Preliminary construction cost indication
  • Financial feasibility
  • Resource allocation & funding
  • Cost study for alternative schemes
  • Benchmarking

Composition

- Preliminary drawings
- Outline Specification / Assumptions
- Scope of Works
- Site Area & Construction Floor Area
- List of Exclusions / Inclusions
- Date of Estimation

Pre-contract Cost Plan Stages

  • Inception & Feasibility Stage
    • Preliminary Cost Estimate
      • Cost/CFA * Functional Area CFA + Preliminaries + Contingencies
  • Outline Proposal Stage
    • Elemental Cost Estimate
      • Elemental Cost * Elemental Quantity + Preliminaries + Contingencies
  • Scheme Design Stage
    • Cost Plan
      • Rate * Approx. Quantity + Preliminaries + Contingencies
  • Design Development Stage
    • Revised Cost Plan
  • Tender
    • Pre-tender Cost Estimate
    • Cost Reconciliation may be required
      • Reflecting cost differencces
      • Cost saving exercise
      • Factors caused cost different
        • Change of site condition
        • Change of design & spec
        • Change of market condition

Exclusions of Estimate

  • Finance and legal expenses
  • Value of land
  • Works outside site boundary
  • Loos furniture & equipment
  • Artwork
  • Professional fees
  • Inflation
  • Management cost of client

Quantity for Cost Plan

CFA & GFA

  • Construction Floor Area (CFA)
    • Measured to the outer face of the external walls
    • Inclusions
      • Lift shaft, stairwell
      • Bay window
      • Balcony and utility platform
  • Gross Floor Area (GFA)
    • Area contained within the external walls of the building measured at each floor level (ncluding any floor below the level of the ground), together with the area of each balcony in the building, which shall be caluculated from the overall dimensions of the balcony (including the thickness of the sides thereof), and the thickness of the external walls of the building.
    • GFA = Site Area * Plot Ratio
    • Exclusion
      • Underground carpark for private use
      • Refuse room, machine room
      • Balcony, utility platform, podium garden
      • Non-structural prefabricated external wall
  • CFA to GFA Ratio
    • 1.05 - 1.10 for residential
    • 1.03 for refuge floor
    • 1.15 - 1.20 for commercial / office
    • 1.25 - 1.40 for hotel

Footprint Area

  • Horizontal area in layout plan, measured from the outside of exterior walls

Key Ratios for Approx. Quantity

  • Facade
    • Measured from drawing
    • Wall to floor ratio
      • 1 : 1 for residential
      • 0.5 : 1 for office
      • 1.5 : 1 for hotel
  • Interal wall area
    • Wall to floor ratio
      • 1 : 1 for residential
      • 0.5 : 1 for office
      • 1.5 : 1 for hotel

    • Concrete

Rates for Cost Plan

General Practice

  • Reasonable assumptions & PC rates
  • Refer to cost data from similar projects
  • Refer to quotations
  • Adjust based on Tender Price Index (TPI)

Factors for Cost Adjustment

  • Project particulars
    • Type (Residential, Commercial, etc.)
    • Grade (High, Medium, Low)
    • Size; Shape; No. of storey
    • Procurement strategy
    • Programme; construction method
  • Site particulars
    • Location
    • Conditions / Constraints (access, working hours, topography, etc.)
  • Market particulars
    • General market change (inflation, deflation)
    • Cost of matieral change
    • Cost of labour change
